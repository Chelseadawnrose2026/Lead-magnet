Interactive Activity

The Oxygen Mask Check

Stand up. Be honest. Step back if true.

Step backward if this is TRUE for you:

  • I have trouble sleeping because my mind won't stop
  • I can't remember the last time I did something for fun
  • I feel guilty when I take time for myself
  • I've snapped at someone I love recently
  • I've said "I'm fine" when I wasn't
  • I'm running on fumes but keep going
  • I've neglected basic self-care because I was "too busy"

The 3 B's of Burnout

Where we hemorrhage our energy

1. BLURRED

Boundaries

Can't tell where you end and others begin

2. BURIED

Needs

Pushing your needs so far down you forget they exist

3. BROKEN

Boundaries

Setting boundaries then immediately violating them

Which B resonates
most with you?

Hold that thought...

Interactive Activity

Boundary Audit

Pair up. 5 minutes each. Listen only, no fixing.

Answer with your partner:

  • What boundary do I KNOW I need to set?
  • What am I afraid will happen if I set it?
  • What is ALREADY happening because I haven't?

The R.E.S.C.U.E.
Framework

Your Personal Rescue Plan

R
RECOGNIZE the warning signs, know YOUR red flags
E
ESTABLISH your non-negotiables, what 2-3 things must you protect?
S
SPEAK your boundaries out loud, a boundary in your head is just a wish
C
CREATE accountability, tell someone, write it down, make it visible
U
UNDERSTAND maintenance is required, reinforce daily
E
EXPECT pushback, people who benefited won't celebrate your new boundaries
Interactive Activity

Your Non-Negotiables

Write THREE things you commit to protect, starting TODAY

Examples:

  • "I will not answer work calls after 8pm"
  • "I will take my full lunch break"
  • "I will ask for help when overwhelmed"

Your turn: Write your 3 non-negotiables now
