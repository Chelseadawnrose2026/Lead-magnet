CHELSEA FLYNN

Catholic Speaker • Life Transformation Coach • Radio Host

"He turns our mess into a message, our tests into testimonies"

Chelsea Flynn is a Catholic convert whose powerful testimony speaks to the heart of anyone searching for belonging, healing, and purpose. Raised without faith in a chaotic home, Chelsea spent years searching for fulfillment in worldly success—only to discover that true transformation comes through Christ.

Her story of walking into a Catholic church and encountering the Father she never knew has moved audiences across parishes, retreats, and conferences. As a mother of three (including a child with autism), founder of Autism Acceptance Cayman Islands, and weekly radio host on BOBO FM 103.1, Chelsea brings authenticity, energy, and deep faith to every engagement.
