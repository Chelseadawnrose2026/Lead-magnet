The Oxygen Mask Check

Boundary Audit

11

The R.E.S.C.U.E.
Framework

Your Personal Rescue Plan

13
R
RECOGNIZE the warning signs

ESTABLISH your non-negotiables
What 2-3 things must you protect?
S
SPEAK your boundaries out loud

14
C
CREATE accountability
Tell someone. Write it down. Make it visible.
U

Reinforce daily
E
